What do I get exactly?

The ActixWeb starter with all the boilerplate code you need to run an online business: a payment system, a database, login, a blog, UI components, and much more.

Is it a website template?

It's more than just a template. You can copy/paste sections to build your site quickly, like a pricing section, an FAQ, and even an entire blog. You also get a bunch of UI components like buttons, modals, popovers, etc.

How often is ActixKit updated?

ActixKit is built for one purpose: to let you launch projects fast, without getting slowed down by constant dependency changes or breaking updates.
